Organic Helper, released under the GNU General Public License, version 3, is a project of Omega Horizon Research. We started from the concept by Mike Bostock. The goal is to make it easier for students and others to build their scientific literacy, specifically within the field of Organic Chemistry.

Omega Horizon Research is a part of Omega Horizon. Omega Horizon was founded by Brian Coe and Nathaniel Buechler.
